FIRE FLAME BUSH

The Fire Flame Bush, also known as Woodfordia fruticosa L., is a small, deciduous shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall. It is commonly found on dry, rocky hillsides, and in open, sunny areas. The Fire Flame Bush has many medicinal and pharmacological uses.

Woodfordia fruticosa

Kingdom:Plantae

- Clade :Angiosperms
- Clade :Eudicots
- Clade :Rosids

- Family:Lythraceae
- Genus :Woodfordia
- Species :W. fruticosa

Uses

- Dried flowers : Help manage liver disorders due to their antioxidant and liver protective properties
- Woodfordins : Help relieve pain and inflammation due to their analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties
- Astringent properties: Help treat skin disorders, superficial wounds, and cuts
