PLANT CATALOGUE

Centre for Science in Society (C-SiS)



Welcome to the virtual catalogue of all plants found in C-SiS (Centre of Science in Society, Cusat). In this catalogue, you can find information on plants contained within C-SiS, in an easy and straightforward manner. The Catalogue is divided into four different categories, that is:

- Trees
- Herbs
- Shrubs
- Creepers and climbers

This divison allows information to be gathered swiftly and efficiently, while also avoiding making a mess out of a single webpage because of too much data. All plants have a page dedicated to themselves to reduce loading time. The links to all categories is shown below.
